# Fulcra illustration guides

These guides describe two related illustration modes:

- [Dark mode](dark-mode.md) is product-native, app-native, and high-contrast. Use it for product explainers, technical diagrams, social assets, and graphics that should feel close to Context.
- [Light mode](light-mode.md) is editorial and presentation-native. Use it for decks, public website sections, comparisons, and diagrams built on white or pale fields.

The modes share topology, semantic color roles, geometry, and the idea of Fulcra as a user-owned context layer between real-world signals and agents. They differ in material treatment: dark mode uses tonal depth and restrained luminosity; light mode uses whitespace, pale grouping fields, and dark ink.

The guides are actionable references. [SOURCES.md](SOURCES.md) explains how their determinations were made.
